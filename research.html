Research

I am a Ph.D. researcher specializing in image segmentation, with applied work spanning medical imaging and corrosion analysis. My background also includes projects in image classification, generative modeling, and NLP classification from my undergraduate research. I have multiple first-author publications, showing a steady record of taking projects from idea to results. Published papers are listed below:
